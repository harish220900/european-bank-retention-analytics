# 🏦 European Bank - Customer Retention Analytics

## Project Overview
Customer Engagement & Product Utilization Analytics for Retention Strategy
using a dataset of 10,000 European Bank customers across France, Germany, and Spain.

## Key Findings
- Overall churn rate: **20.4%**
- Active members churn at **14.3%** vs **26.9%** for inactive members
- 2-product customers have the lowest churn at **7.6%**
- Germany has the highest regional churn at **32.4%**
- 2,356 high-value inactive customers face **32.8%** churn risk
